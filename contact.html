Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

What are your office hours?

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The Admissions Office is also open on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. All offices are closed on Sundays and school holidays.

How do I schedule a campus tour?

Campus tours are available Tuesday and Thursday mornings from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM. You can schedule a tour by contacting our Admissions Office at (555) 123-4568 or by filling out the tour request form on our Admissions page.

Who should I contact about financial aid?

For questions about financial aid, please contact Thomas Anderson, our Financial Aid Director, at tanderson@isstsds.edu or (555) 123-4582. You can also find detailed information about our financial aid process on the Admissions page.

How do I report a student absence?

To report a student absence, please call our attendance line at (555) 123-4590 before 8:30 AM on the day of the absence. You can also report an absence through the Parent Portal or by emailing attendance@isstsds.edu.

How can I apply for a teaching position?

All current job openings are listed on the Careers page of our website. To apply, please submit your resume, cover letter, and teaching philosophy to careers@isstsds.edu. For specific questions about employment opportunities, contact our Human Resources department at (555) 123-4587.
