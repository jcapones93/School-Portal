Student Life

At Brightfield Academy, we believe in educating the whole student through a rich variety of extracurricular activities and community experiences.

Life Beyond the Classroom

A Vibrant Community

Brightfield Academy offers a wide range of extracurricular activities that complement our academic program and help students discover their passions, develop new skills, and build lasting friendships.

Our dedicated faculty advisors and coaches guide students as they explore interests, take on leadership roles, and contribute to our school community. We encourage all students to participate in at least one activity each semester.

School Traditions

Annual events that bring our community together and create lasting memories.

September

Welcome Back Festival

Our school year kicks off with a community celebration featuring food, games, and performances. Students and families reconnect and welcome new members to our community.

October

Fall Arts Festival

A showcase of student creativity through visual arts, music, drama, and dance performances. The festival transforms our campus into an immersive arts experience.

December

Winter Concert & Giving Drive

Our music ensembles perform seasonal favorites while the school community comes together to support local families in need through our annual giving drive.

February

Science & Innovation Fair

Students showcase their research projects and innovative solutions to real-world problems. The fair includes demonstrations, guest speakers, and interactive exhibits.

May

Spring Carnival

A day of outdoor fun with games, rides, food, and entertainment. This student-led event raises funds for student activities and local charities.

June

Graduation & Senior Celebration

We celebrate our graduating seniors with a formal ceremony followed by an all-night celebration with activities, entertainment, and special traditions.

Student Support

Resources and programs to support student well-being and success.

Advisory Program

Each student is assigned to a faculty advisor who provides academic guidance, personal support, and serves as a liaison between school and home.

  • Small advisory groups meet regularly
  • Individual check-ins and goal setting
  • Four-year relationships with advisors
  • Community-building activities

Counseling Services

Our counseling team provides social-emotional support, academic guidance, and resources to help students navigate challenges and thrive.

  • Individual counseling
  • Group support sessions
  • Wellness workshops
  • Crisis intervention

Peer Support Network

Trained student leaders provide peer-to-peer support, mentoring, and resources to foster a positive school culture.

  • New student orientation guides
  • Peer tutoring program
  • Conflict resolution
  • Community-building initiatives
