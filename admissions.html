Admissions Process

Our thoughtful admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging and supportive environment.

1

Inquire

Begin by exploring our website, attending an information session, or contacting our admissions office to learn more about our programs and community.

Request Information
2

Visit

Schedule a campus tour to experience our vibrant community firsthand. Meet with faculty, observe classes, and speak with current students.

Schedule a Visit
3

Apply

Complete our online application, including student and parent statements, teacher recommendations, and academic records.

Begin Application
4

Interview

Participate in a personal interview with a member of our admissions team. This is an opportunity for us to get to know you better and for you to ask questions.

Learn More
5

Decision

Admissions decisions are released according to our notification timeline. Accepted students will receive enrollment information and next steps.

View Timeline
